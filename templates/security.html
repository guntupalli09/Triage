Security

Your documents are never stored

Triage is designed from the ground up so that your confidential documents never persist on our servers.

256-bit TLS Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Every connection between your browser and Triage is secured end-to-end.

Zero Document Storage

Uploaded documents are processed in memory and immediately discarded. We store only the structured analysis results — never the original file.

No AI Training on Your Data

Your documents are never used to train AI models. The LLM explanation layer receives only extracted clause text — never full documents.

Deterministic Audit Trail

Every finding is produced by a deterministic rule with a traceable rule ID. The same document always produces the same results — no probabilistic variance.

Automatic Data Deletion

Analysis metadata is retained only while your account is active. You can delete any contract record at any time, and all associated data is permanently removed.

Privacy & Compliance

Session-based authentication with PBKDF2 password hashing and HttpOnly cookies. All passwords are salted and hashed — never stored in plain text.
