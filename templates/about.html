About

Built on research, not hype

TriageCounsel was built for small and mid-sized law firms to solve a specific problem: AI contract tools make decisions they can't explain. We separated the two concerns — deterministic rules decide, AI explains.

Our Approach

Most AI contract tools treat the entire analysis as a single LLM prompt. This makes results non-deterministic, non-auditable, and impossible to verify.

TriageCounsel decomposes the problem into two layers: a deterministic rule engine that identifies risks using pattern matching and structural analysis, and an LLM explanation layer that translates findings into plain language.

Peer-Reviewed

TriageCounsel's dual-layer architecture was peer-reviewed and accepted at the International Conference on Computational Science (ICCS 2026).

The research demonstrates that deterministic rules with AI explanations achieve higher accuracy, consistency, and auditability than end-to-end LLM approaches.

Our Mission

Make contract risk analysis deterministic, auditable, and accessible — so small and mid-sized law firms can trust the output and focus on what matters.
