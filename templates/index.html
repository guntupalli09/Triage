How It Works

1

Deterministic Engine Flags Patterns

Our rule engine uses regex and proximity matching to detect exact clause patterns. Every finding includes matched excerpts for full transparency and auditability.

2

AI explains why detected patterns may matter and suggests negotiation levers. The LLM never invents risks—it only explains what the deterministic engine found.

3

You receive a structured report highlighting risk indicators, matched excerpts, and suggested negotiation points. Review with qualified legal counsel before signing.

Why This Is Reliable

  • Deterministic first-pass prevents hallucinations about clause presence. All risk detection is rule-based and auditable.
  • Matched excerpts shown for every finding, so you can verify exactly what triggered each rule.
  • Version-controlled rules ensure consistent, reproducible analysis across all contracts.
