# Triage AI — ICP & Commission Partner Strategy

## Part 1: Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Who Gets the Most Value from Triage AI?

Triage AI solves one problem: **reducing the time and cost of first-pass contract risk review**. The ICP is anyone who reviews contracts frequently, can't afford (or doesn't want to pay) a lawyer for every one, and needs defensible, auditable output.

---

### Tier 1 — Highest-Value Customers (Unlimited plan @ $249/mo)

| Segment | Why They Pay | Pain Point |
|---|---|---|
| **In-house legal teams at mid-market companies (50–500 employees)** | Review 20–100+ contracts/month (vendor MSAs, NDAs, procurement). A junior paralegal costs $4,000+/mo; Triage does first-pass for $249/mo. | Volume overwhelms limited legal headcount. |
| **Procurement / vendor-management teams** | Every new vendor means an MSA. They need standardized risk screening before routing to legal. | Legal bottleneck slows procurement cycles. |
| **M&A / deal-room teams** | Due diligence requires triaging dozens of contracts in a deal room within days. Batch upload of 50 files is the killer feature. | Tight deal timelines, high contract volume. |
| **Law firms (small to mid-size)** | Use Triage as an internal accelerator — triage client contracts before assigning associate time. | Associate hours are expensive; first-pass triage can be automated. |

**Annual revenue per customer**: $2,988/year
**Commission per referral (25%)**: **$747/year per client**

---

### Tier 2 — Growth Customers (Professional plan @ $49 one-time)

| Segment | Why They Pay | Pain Point |
|---|---|---|
| **Startup founders (seed to Series B)** | Sign 5–15 NDAs and MSAs/month. Can't afford $500–$2,000/contract for legal review. | Cash-constrained but contract-heavy. |
| **Freelancers & independent consultants** | Receive client MSAs they can't afford to review. Need to spot bad IP assignment, non-competes, indemnification. | Zero legal budget, high contract risk. |
| **Real estate operators / property managers** | Vendor agreements, contractor MSAs, confidentiality agreements. | Frequent low-complexity contracts, no in-house counsel. |
| **Franchise operators** | Review franchise agreements and vendor contracts. | Non-negotiable contracts but need to understand risk exposure. |

### Tier 3 — Entry Customers (Starter plan @ $4.99 one-time)

| Segment | Why They Pay |
|---|---|
| Individual professionals evaluating the tool | Low commitment trial |
| Students / researchers studying contract risk | Affordable access |
| Occasional users (1–2 contracts/year) | One-off need |

### ICP Summary — The Person Who Writes the Check

> **Primary buyer**: Head of Legal, General Counsel, VP of Procurement, or COO at a company with 50–500 employees that processes 20+ contracts/month and doesn't have enough legal headcount to review them all.
>
> **Secondary buyer**: Startup founder or freelancer who signs contracts regularly and can't justify $500+ per legal review.

## Part 4: LinkedIn Prospecting — How to Find These Partners

### Recommended Approach: LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Automated Outreach

**Do NOT scrape LinkedIn directly** — it violates LinkedIn's Terms of Service, gets your account banned, and the data quality is poor. Instead, use these legitimate tools:

### Tool Stack (Recommended)

| Tool | Purpose | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** | Advanced search, lead lists, InMail | ~$99/mo | The only reliable way to search LinkedIn by title, company size, industry. Required foundation. |
| **Apollo.io** | Find emails + LinkedIn profiles matching your ICP filters | Free tier available, $49/mo for full | Has 270M+ contacts. Search by title, industry, company size. Export with emails. Compliant. |
| **Instantly.ai** or **Smartlead** | Cold email outreach sequences | ~$30–97/mo | Send personalized email sequences to exported Apollo leads. High deliverability. |
| **Phantombuster** | LinkedIn automation (profile visits, connection requests, message sequences) | $69/mo | Automates LinkedIn outreach at scale. Use carefully — stay under 100 actions/day. |
| **PartnerStack** or **Rewardful** | Affiliate/partner program management | $0–500/mo | Tracks referral links, calculates commissions, handles payouts. Essential for scaling. |

### Step-by-Step Playbook

#### Step 1: Set Up Partner Program Infrastructure
- Create a landing page at `triage-gamma.vercel.app/partners`
- Use **Rewardful** (integrates with Stripe) to generate unique referral links
- Set commission to 25% recurring on Unlimited plan

#### Step 2: Build Lead Lists in Apollo.io
Use these search filters:

**Search 1 — Fractional GCs & Legal Consultants**
- Title: "Fractional General Counsel" OR "Legal Consultant" OR "Contract Advisor" OR "Fractional CLO"
- Industry: Legal Services, Business Consulting
- Company size: 1–10 (they're independent)
- Location: United States

**Search 2 — Startup Advisors**
- Title: "Startup Advisor" OR "Fractional COO" OR "Fractional CTO" OR "Operations Consultant"
- Industry: Startup, Technology, Business Consulting
- Company size: 1–50
- Location: United States

**Search 3 — Legal Tech Influencers**
- Title: "Legal Tech" OR "LegalOps" OR "Legal Innovation" OR "Legal Technology"
- Seniority: Director, VP, C-suite
- Location: United States, UK, Canada

**Search 4 — Accelerator/Incubator People**
- Title: "Accelerator" OR "Incubator" OR "Portfolio Manager" OR "EIR"
- Industry: Venture Capital, Startup
- Location: United States

**Search 5 — M&A / Business Brokers**
- Title: "Business Broker" OR "M&A Advisor" OR "Deal Advisory"
- Industry: Financial Services, Business Consulting
- Location: United States

If you want to programmatically identify potential partners from **public LinkedIn data** for research purposes, the script in `partners/linkedin_prospector.py` uses Apollo.io's API (which is compliant and legal) to find matching profiles.
