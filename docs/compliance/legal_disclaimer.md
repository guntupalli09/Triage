# Legal Disclaimer

## Core Disclaimer

**This tool provides automated risk triage, not legal advice.**

This is the fundamental limitation that governs all system behavior and communications.

## What This Means

### ✅ What the Tool Provides

- Automated risk detection using deterministic rules
- Business-friendly explanations of detected risks
- Suggestions for commonly negotiated sections
- Risk prioritization to help focus legal review

### ❌ What the Tool Does NOT Provide

- Legal advice or legal opinions
- Enforceability determinations
- Jurisdiction-specific legal analysis
- Recommendations to sign or not sign contracts
- Replacement for qualified legal counsel

## Language Constraints

The system uses conservative, non-advice language:

### ✅ Permitted Language

- "may indicate risk"
- "can increase exposure"
- "commonly negotiated"
- "you may want to confirm"
- "may create burden"

### ❌ Prohibited Language

- "safe to sign"
- "illegal"
- "enforceable" or "unenforceable"
- "you should" or "you must"
- "guaranteed" or "certain"

These constraints are enforced in:
- LLM prompts
- Output validation
- Template displays
- All user-facing communications

## User Responsibilities

### Required Actions

Users must:
1. **Consult legal counsel**: Always get legal review before signing contracts
2. **Understand limitations**: Know what tool does and does not do
3. **Make own decisions**: Tool does not make decisions for users
4. **Verify findings**: Confirm findings with legal professionals

### Prohibited Uses

Users must NOT:
1. **Rely solely on tool**: Never skip legal review based on tool output
2. **Use as legal advice**: Tool is not a replacement for legal counsel
3. **Make legal claims**: Do not use tool output as legal authority
4. **Skip due diligence**: Tool does not replace proper contract review

## System Limitations

### Detection Limitations

- **False positives possible**: May flag standard clauses as risky
- **Not comprehensive**: Only detects predefined risk patterns
- **Pattern-based**: Cannot understand context or intent
- **Scope limited**: Optimized for NDAs and MSAs only

### Explanation Limitations

- **LLM dependency**: Explanations depend on API availability
- **Quality variance**: Explanation quality may vary
- **Suggestions only**: Missing sections are suggestions, not detections

### Technical Limitations

- **File format support**: Only PDF, DOCX, TXT
- **Text extraction**: May be imperfect for complex layouts
- **Session storage**: Ephemeral, lost on server restart
