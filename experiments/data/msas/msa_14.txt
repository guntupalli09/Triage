MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date. A late payment fee of 4% applies to overdue amounts.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Provider grants Client a royalty-free, perpetual license to use deliverables for Client's business operations.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Mutual indemnification: each party indemnifies the other for its own negligence, up to $1,000,000 per claim.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Each party's liability is capped at the greater of $500,000 or the annual contract value.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 60 days written notice for any reason.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations survive termination and continue for 4 years.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider provides services 'as-is' with no warranties except as expressly stated.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through mediation, then arbitration if mediation is unsuccessful.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by Florida state law.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
The prevailing party in any dispute shall recover reasonable attorneys' fees from the non-prevailing party.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
