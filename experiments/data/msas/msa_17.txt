MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment terms are specified in each Statement of Work. Late payments incur a late fee of 6% of the overdue amount.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All intellectual property developed during the term of this Agreement shall be jointly owned by Provider and Client, with each party having the right to use and license such IP.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Provider agrees to indemnify Client for third-party claims arising from Provider's gross negligence, up to a maximum of $750,000 per claim.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's liability is capped at the total contract value, except for indemnification obligations which are unlimited.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach by the other party.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations survive termination and continue for 6 years.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider provides services on an 'as-is' basis with no warranties, express or implied.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through mediation, and if unsuccessful, through binding arbitration.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by Arizona state law.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
The prevailing party in any legal proceeding shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
