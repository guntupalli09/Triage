MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Provider monthly in advance based on the service schedule.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Provider grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use deliverables for Client's internal business purposes only.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Mutual indemnification: each party indemnifies the other for claims arising from its own negligence, capped at the annual contract value.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Liability is capped at the greater of $1,000,000 or the annual contract value, except for indemnification obligations.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
Either party may terminate for convenience with 90 days written notice.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations continue for 5 years after termination.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider provides services on an 'as-is' basis with no warranties.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through good faith negotiation.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is subject to the laws of the State of Texas.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
Each party bears its own legal costs.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
