MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment is due within 40 days of invoice date. Late payments incur interest at 1% per month.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Provider grants Client a worldwide, perpetual, non-exclusive license to use deliverables for Client's internal business purposes.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Mutual indemnification: each party indemnifies the other for claims arising from its own breach, capped at the annual contract value.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Each party's liability is limited to the total fees paid in the 24 months preceding the claim.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 45 days written notice for convenience.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations continue for 2 years after termination of this Agreement.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider warrants that services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through direct negotiation between the parties' authorized representatives.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Nevada.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
Each party shall pay its own attorneys' fees and costs in connection with any dispute.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
