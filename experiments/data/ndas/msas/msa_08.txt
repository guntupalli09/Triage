MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Provider within 20 days of invoice date. A late fee of 5% applies to overdue amounts.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product created under this Agreement shall be assigned to Client. Provider hereby assigns all right, title, and interest.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Provider shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client from all claims, losses, and expenses, without limit.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Notwithstanding any other provision, Provider's liability is unlimited for indemnification and confidentiality breaches.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations are perpetual and survive termination indefinitely.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider provides services on an 'as-is' basis with no express or implied warranties.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes shall be resolved exclusively in the courts of Delaware.

10. GOVERNING LAW
Delaware law governs this Agreement.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
The prevailing party in any legal action shall recover reasonable attorneys' fees from the other party.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
