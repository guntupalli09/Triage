MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment terms are specified in each Statement of Work. Late payments accrue interest at 2% per month.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property. Client receives a limited license for deliverables.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Each party indemnifies the other to the extent required by applicable law for claims arising from its own breach.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Each party's liability is limited to the annual contract value, except as required by law.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 45 days written notice for convenience.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations continue for 4 years after termination.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider warrants services will be performed in accordance with industry standards.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through good faith negotiation, then mediation if needed.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is subject to New York state law.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
Each party bears its own legal costs unless the other party prevails.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
