1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide the services described in attached Statements of Work.

2. PAYMENT TERMS

Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property. Client receives a limited, non-exclusive license for deliverables.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Each party shall indemnify the other to the extent required by applicable law for claims arising from its own breach.

Each party's liability is limited to the total fees paid under this Agreement in the preceding calendar year.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement may be terminated by mutual written consent or by either party with 90 days notice.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Confidentiality obligations continue for 3 years after termination of this Agreement.

8. WARRANTIES
Provider warrants compliance with all applicable laws and regulations in performing services.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through good faith negotiation between authorized representatives.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is subject to Oregon state law.

11. ATTORNEYS' FEES
Each party bears its own legal expenses in connection with any dispute.

12. MODIFICATIONS
This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
