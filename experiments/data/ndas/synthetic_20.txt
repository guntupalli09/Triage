Confidential Information means all non-public information disclosed by one party to the other.

The Receiving Party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information.

3. TERM
This Agreement shall remain in effect in perpetuity with no expiration date.

4. INDEMNIFICATION

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Limitation of liability shall not apply to indemnification, except as required by applicable law.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
The Receiving Party grants a non-exclusive license to use the work product.

7. ATTORNEYS' FEES
Each party shall pay its own attorneys' fees unless the other party prevails.

8. RENEWAL
This Agreement may be renewed by mutual written consent.

9. AUDIT RIGHTS
No audit rights are granted under this Agreement.

10. LATE FEES
No late fees apply.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by applicable law.

12. DEFINITIONS
Confidential Information means information marked as confidential.
